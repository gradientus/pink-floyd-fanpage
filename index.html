Pink Floyd

Bio:

Pink Floyd formed in London in 1965. They are known for their progressive and psychedelic rock music.

They made some kick-ass music, but they hated each other. For over two decades the members of the band fought, waged legal war, and were generally assy with each other.

My favorite albums are The Division Bell, The Dark Side of the Moon, and A Momentary Lapse of Reason.

Yes, you can sync The Dark Side of the Moon with The Wizard of Oz. Just start playing when the MGM lion roars the 3rd time.

You can also sync The Division Bell, starting when the movie switches to color.

The band members were (not sure if you can include Bob Klose or not, but I included him):

  • Roger Waters
  • David Gilmour
  • Nick Mason
  • Richard Wright
  • Syd Barrett
  • Bob Klose
