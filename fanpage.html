Pink Floyd

Bio:

Pink Floyd is an English progressive, phychedelic rock band formed in London in 1965 by Syd Barret, Roger Waters, Nick Mason, and Richard Wright. They made some kick-ass music in the 50+ years they have been together, strangely they hate each other. The band has physically fought, waged legal war, and maligned each other since the 80s.

The band members included:

  • Roger Waters
  • David Gilmour
  • Nick Mason
  • Richard Wright
  • Syd Barrett
  • Bob Klose

Sadly, Syd Barret and Richard Wright have passed away.
